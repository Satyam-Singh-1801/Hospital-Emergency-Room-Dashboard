## Project Overview

The Hospital Emergency Room Analysis Dashboard is a Power BI project developed to analyze emergency room operations and healthcare performance using interactive dashboards and KPI visualizations. The project tracks patient flow, wait times, admissions, referrals, and patient satisfaction to help improve operational efficiency and decision-making through data-driven insights.

# Objectives

Analyze hospital emergency room operations using Power BI
Monitor patient flow and emergency room performance
Track important KPIs such as wait time, admissions, and patient satisfaction
Identify peak patient traffic hours and operational bottlenecks
Analyze department referrals and patient demographics
Improve healthcare decision-making through data-driven insights
Create interactive and user-friendly dashboards for better reporting
Optimize resource management and emergency room efficiency

# Process

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard was developed through a structured data analytics and business intelligence workflow to ensure accurate analysis and meaningful insights.

1. Requirement Gathering

Collected business requirements and identified key objectives, KPIs, and reporting needs for emergency room analysis.

2. Data Collection & Understanding

Reviewed the hospital emergency room dataset to understand available fields such as patient details, wait times, admissions, referrals, and satisfaction scores.

3. Data Connection

Imported the dataset into Power BI from Excel/CSV sources for analysis and visualization.

4. Data Cleaning & Transformation

Used Power Query to clean the dataset by handling missing values, correcting data formats, removing duplicates, and preparing the data for analysis.

5. Data Modeling

Created relationships between tables and structured the data model to improve reporting efficiency and performance.

6. DAX Calculations

Implemented DAX measures and calculated columns to create KPIs such as:

Total Patients
Average Wait Time
Satisfaction Score
Admission Rate
Referral Analysis
7. Dashboard Design & Layout

Designed a user-friendly and professional dashboard layout to improve readability and user interaction.

8. Data Visualization Development

Created interactive charts, graphs, KPI cards, and filters to analyze:

Patient flow
Department referrals
Demographics
Wait times
Emergency room trends
9. Insights Generation

Analyzed dashboard results to identify operational patterns, peak hours, bottlenecks, and healthcare performance trends.

10. Final Dashboard & Reporting

Developed final interactive dashboards including:

Monthly View
Consolidated View
Patient Details
Key Takeaways Dashboard

The final solution provides actionable insights for improving emergency room efficiency and healthcare decision-making.

## Project Insights

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard generated several valuable insights that can help improve healthcare operations and patient management:

Identified peak emergency room hours and high patient traffic periods, helping hospitals optimize staffing and resource allocation.
Analyzed average patient wait times to detect operational delays and improve emergency response efficiency.
Monitored the percentage of patients attended within 30 minutes to evaluate service timeliness and healthcare performance.
Evaluated patient satisfaction scores to understand service quality trends and identify areas requiring improvement.
Identified departments with high referral rates, enabling better resource planning and workload distribution.
Analyzed patient demographics including age, gender, and race to understand patient distribution patterns.
Compared admitted vs non-admitted patients to evaluate emergency room utilization and operational efficiency.
Tracked monthly performance trends to identify patterns, seasonal variations, and operational bottlenecks.
Generated actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making, improve patient care, and enhance emergency room management.

## Final Conclusion

The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard effectively analyzed emergency room operations using Power BI by tracking key healthcare KPIs such as patient flow, wait times, admissions, referrals, and satisfaction scores.
